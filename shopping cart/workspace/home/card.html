Lion

As well as attracting females, their manes may also protect their neck and head from injuries during fights

  • Scientific Name:Panthera Leo
  • Top Speed: 50mph
  • Length: 10ft
  • Life Span: Males (8-10)yrs

Lions are the only known cat species where individuals roar together - with even young cubs joining in. The roaring usually lasts about 40 seconds. A group of lions, also called 'a pride', often roar together to mark their territory - a roar can be heard from 5 miles away.
